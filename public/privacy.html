Privacy Policy

Zaney Links is a browser-based word game. The site is designed to work without an account, login, or player profile.

Information We Collect

Zaney Links does not ask for your name, email address, password, payment details, or precise location. Basic technical information may be processed automatically by hosting, security, analytics, or advertising providers, such as device type, browser type, pages visited, approximate region, and timestamps.

Game And Puzzle Data

Game play happens in your browser. Custom puzzles are encoded into the share link itself. Anyone with a custom puzzle link can open the puzzle and read its words, categories, and explanations.

Cookies And Advertising

If advertising is enabled, Google AdSense and related Google services may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve, limit, personalize, and measure ads. Google may use data collected on this site together with data from other sites and apps.

You can learn more about how Google uses advertising data at policies.google.com/technologies/ads and manage ad personalization at adssettings.google.com.

Analytics

Zaney Links may use privacy-conscious analytics to understand aggregate usage, such as which packs are played and whether puzzles are completed. Analytics should not be used to collect custom puzzle contents unless a player intentionally submits a puzzle for review.

Children

Zaney Links is a general-audience puzzle game and is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can avoid custom puzzle sharing by not sending custom puzzle links. You can also clear your browser data to remove locally stored site data. Browser and device settings may let you block or limit cookies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@zaneylinks.com.
